# Code of Conduct

Agent-Hub follows the Contributor Covenant principles: be respectful,
constructive, and considerate of different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints.
- Focus critique on code, design, documentation, and behavior.
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and correct them.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Disruption of project spaces or issue discussions.
- Abuse of project infrastructure.

Maintainers may moderate issues, pull requests, discussions, and contributions
to keep the project healthy and useful.
