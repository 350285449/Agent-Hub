# Compressed Mechanism Final Assessment

Scope: cloud models only. No new theories, primitive searches, or large theory tournaments.

## Summary

The compressed mechanism is:

`Evidence -> Grounding -> Branch Commitment -> Outcome`

The current Agent-Hub evidence supports this as the best compressed explanation of outcomes. It explains why prior theories collapsed, why static pre-run predictors underperform prospectively, why grounding is the strongest minimal signal, and why branch commitment becomes decisive around mid-execution.

## Final Questions

1. Is the compressed mechanism sufficient?

Yes, for explaining most surviving cloud-only outcome signal. It is sufficient as a runtime diagnostic/explanatory mechanism, not as a proven causal intervention law.

2. Does it outperform previous theories?

Yes. Previous theory families either collapse into Grounding Integrity or execution trajectories. After core controls and family controls, independent theory gains are zero or negative. The compressed mechanism preserves the useful signal without the extra theory structure.

3. What is the smallest useful model?

`evidence recognition + grounded-action ratio + branch commitment quality`

For operational use, include commitment timing as a warning clock, but the smallest explanatory model is recognized evidence converted into grounded action before commitment quality is decided.

4. Is Grounding upstream of Commitment?

Yes, in the best current operational direction. First grounding event is the strongest predictive event, and branch collapse is best interpreted as the downstream lock-in point. Commitment without grounding is often false or premature commitment.

5. Is Branch Commitment the final bottleneck?

Yes, with a caveat. Branch Commitment is the final bottleneck once the run has enough evidence and grounding to choose a path. But the more fundamental bottleneck is whether the committed branch is grounded. Commitment is decisive because it makes the grounding success or failure hard to reverse.

6. Is this the best current candidate for a fundamental mechanism?

Yes, with restrained language. It is the best current candidate mechanism in the cloud-only Agent-Hub corpus. It should be called a candidate fundamental mechanism, not a proven law, because delivered intervention causality and full cross-family universality remain incomplete.

## Evidence Balance

Evidence for:

- Grounding event success gap is very large.
- Grounded-action ratio is the strongest single grounding integrity metric.
- The minimal grounding group recovers most surviving dynamic signal.
- Material predictability appears around the 50% commitment prefix.
- Prior theory families collapse into grounding/trajectory controls.
- Dominant failures are misinterpretation and evidence-action disconnect, not raw evidence absence.

Evidence against overclaiming:

- Prospective reconstruction is positive but modest.
- Randomized delivered intervention causality is not proven.
- Benchmark-level stability remains uneven.
- Agentic and research family coverage is weaker than coding/reasoning coverage.
- Commitment timing is near 50% aggregate but not universal.

## Final Verdict

D. Candidate fundamental mechanism.

The compressed mechanism is the strongest current candidate because it is small, empirically grounded, and explains the collapse of previous theories. It remains a candidate rather than a settled law until live delivered interventions show that repairing grounding before commitment causally improves outcomes.
